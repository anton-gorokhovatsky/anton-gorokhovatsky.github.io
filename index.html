Welcome to the Garage Digital program, a collaboration between the Garage Museum of Contemporary Art and leading artists, scientists, programmers, and art historians. Our goal is to explore and support new languages of visual culture arising from the influence of the latest technologies and media on everyday life and art and research practice.

Virtual Reality Art

Join us for a series of workshops and exhibitions featuring artists working with virtual reality technology. Learn how to create immersive, interactive artworks and engage with this exciting new medium.

Artificial Intelligence in Art

Explore the intersection of art and artificial intelligence with experts in the field. Participate in talks, demonstrations, and hands-on workshops to learn how AI is transforming the creative process.

Digital Fabrication

Join us for a series of workshops and demonstrations featuring artists and designers using digital fabrication techniques such as 3D printing and laser cutting. Learn how to use these tools to create innovative artworks and prototypes.
